Orange Granita

A simple and refreshing dessert

A granita is made by boiling sugar and water, adding fruit juice or puree, and stirring occasionally while freezing into a delicious slushy ice crystal texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh squeezed orange juice (roughly 6 oranges worth)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1oz Cointreau or Liqor 43 (optional)

Directions

  1. Make a simple syrup by simmering your sugar with water until clear. Let cool.
  2. Mix your simple syrup with your juice and optional liquor.
  3. Pour into a flat bottomed, freezer ready dish.
  4. Place in your freezer and stir every 20 minutes or so, being sure to scrape any forming ice crystals off the sides and bottom. repeat until you have a nice slushy consistency.
  5. Serve immediately in small dessert cups or bowls, or in your hollowed orange peels.
